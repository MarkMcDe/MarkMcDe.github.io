MUSEUM of SCIENCE and INDUSTRY

Chicago, Illinois

  The Museum of Science and Industry keeps four machines running, usually at the bottom of their main stairwells. This museum once tried to offer a reproduction of Colleen Moore's Fairy Castle, a longtime popular exhibit, but the mold was too complex to use.
